01 Problem Statement
Outcomes Dashboard · Revamp
Where we started

The old dashboard tracked activity, not outcomes.

Teams could see how many calls were dialed and connected — but couldn't answer the question that actually matters: "Did we qualify or reactivate any leads?"

Before · Old Dashboard
What was missing
!
No outcome metrics Only "Dialed" and "Connected" — no view into leads qualified or reactivated.
!
No efficiency signals No avg attempts-per-contact to gauge how hard the agent is working per lead.
!
No "when to call" insights No heatmap or peak-hour data — teams had to guess the best calling windows.
!
No agent-level goals Per-agent view showed call counts only — no success rate against an LQ or LR goal.
!
No period comparisons No deltas vs prior period or YoY — impossible to tell if performance was improving.
